Your Questions, Answered

Who is this for?

Sam Brands' Content Engine is built for coaches, consultants, creators, and business owners who are already generating revenue — ideally $10,000 or more per month consistently — and want to turn their personal brand into a predictable source of leads and clients. If you're still in the early stages of figuring out your offer or your business model, our free Discord community is a better starting point. The Content Engine is designed for people who are ready to scale what's already working, not build from scratch.

What exactly do you do?

We handle your entire content production system. That starts with strategy — figuring out what to say, who to say it to, and how to structure your offer so your content actually drives business. Then we work with you on scripting and hooks before filming, so you're not just talking to a camera and hoping something lands. Once you film your raw footage, our team takes over completely: editing your long-form video, cutting short-form clips for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok, designing thumbnails, writing captions, and scheduling everything. You show up, film, and we handle the rest.

Do I have to film the content myself?

For Tier 1 and Tier 2 clients, yes — you film the raw footage and we produce everything from it. For Tier 3 clients, we offer an optional AI digital twin add-on powered by HeyGen, which allows us to produce additional content using your likeness and voice for weeks when you're traveling, too busy to film, or want to scale output beyond what filming alone allows. This is an optional addition, not a replacement for real content — authentic footage will always be your primary content.

How long does it take to get started?

Once your application is approved, your Growth Audit call is complete, and your first payment is processed, most clients see their first content batch within 1-2 weeks. The speed of onboarding depends largely on how quickly you can provide raw footage and brand assets — the faster we get what we need, the faster we start producing.

What platforms do you post on?

We post on all major platforms — both short-form (TikToks, Reels, Shorts) and long-form (YouTube). We always recommend leading with YouTube as the primary platform since long-form video builds the deepest trust with your audience and gives us the most raw material to repurpose everywhere else.

Will the content sound and feel like me?

Yes — and this is something we take seriously. During onboarding we run a full brand voice and strategy session to understand how you talk, what you care about, and how your audience thinks. We also work with you on scripting and hooks before filming, so the raw footage itself is already in your voice. Our editing and strategy work refines what you give us — it doesn't replace your personality with a generic template.

Do you guarantee results like follower growth or more leads?

No — and we think you should be skeptical of any agency that does. We guarantee the deliverables: the exact content volume outlined in your tier, delivered every month. We don't guarantee specific business outcomes like follower counts, lead numbers, or revenue, because those depend on factors outside our control — your offer quality, your market, platform algorithm changes, and how well your content resonates with your specific audience. What we can tell you is that our system is built on the same approach that generated $500,000 from a YouTube channel with only 9,580 subscribers — consistent, avatar-focused content that speaks directly to the right people, not just content that chases views.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. Everything is month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime with 7 days written notice. We keep clients because the work delivers results, not because they're locked in.

What happens if you don't deliver the agreed content?

If we fall short of the content volume outlined in your tier for any given month, you receive a prorated credit toward the following month or a refund for the shortfall — whichever you prefer. This is our deliverable guarantee, and it's in writing in your Service Agreement.

How do strategy calls work?

Strategy calls are scheduled recurring calls included in every tier — monthly for Tier 1, bi-weekly for Tier 2, and weekly for Tier 3. These aren't just check-ins. We use them to review what's performing, adjust your content direction based on real data, work through offer positioning, and make sure your content is actually tied to your business goals rather than just producing content for content's sake.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, and most clients do. Many start on Foundation or Growth to see how the system works with their brand, then move up as their content volume needs grow or they want more hands-on strategy involvement. Upgrades take effect at the start of the next billing period.

What do I need to have ready before we start?

Ideally you should have a clear offer (what you sell and who you sell it to), a basic understanding of your target audience, and the ability to film yourself on a decent camera or smartphone. You don't need a professional studio setup to start — we'll work with what you have and help you improve it over time. You'll also need to provide access to your social media accounts and any existing brand assets (logo, colors, fonts) so we can keep everything visually consistent.

How do I apply?

Click the "Book your Growth Audit" button on this page, fill out our short application, and if it looks like a good fit, you'll be directed to book a 30-minute call. On that call we'll look at your business and personal brand together, figure out what working with us would look like, and answer any questions you have. We only move forward if it makes sense for both sides.

What if I'm not ready for a paid service yet?

Join our free Discord community. We run free weekly group coaching calls, share resources and templates, and build real relationships before ever asking for anything. When you're ready to scale, we'll be there.
